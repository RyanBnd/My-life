From Kindergarten to University

My education journey began at a small nursery school in my village, where I learned the basics of reading, writing, and counting. I later joined primary school at Tututha and passed my KCPE with determination and discipline.

I went to high school at Imale school where I discovered my love for technology and problem-solving. After KCSE results, I pursued a certificate, diploma, degree in Bachelor of business Information Technology at Zetech, where I’m currently completing my degree.

Some of the extracurricular activities I’ve enjoyed include:

  • Debate and public speaking clubs
  • Fight club(Taekwondo and boxing)
  • School musical events
